# Core Features

WeSwap goes beyond standard trading by offering powerful modes that allow investors to automate market opportunities.

### 1. The Boomerang Mode (Signature Feature)

We are introducing a game-changer. Boomerang Mode allows you to capitalize on short-term opportunities without risking your long-term holdings concept.

* **The Logic:** It establishes an automated loop between your "WSP Coin" (your main holding) and a "Target Coin" (the opportunity).
* **How It Works:**
  1. **Input:** Select your main holding (e.g., `WSP` or `USDC`) as the "WSP Coin".
  2. **Target:** Identify a hype token as the "Target Coin".
  3. **Trigger:** The system automatically buys the Target Coin when it hits a specific Price or Market Cap.
  4. **Return:** Once the Target Coin hits your Take-Profit goal, the system sells it and automatically swaps everything back into your **original coin** (WSP Coin).
* **Result:** You effectively increase the amount of your main holding in your wallet.

### 2. Smart Limit Orders

Most DEXs on Solana only execute at market price. WeSwap brings the CEX (Centralized Exchange) experience to the DEX world.

* **Buy/Sell Orders:** You can place automated buy or sell orders at your specified price without needing to monitor the charts 24/7.
* **Slippage Protection:** Offers optimized slippage settings to protect you from heavy losses during volatile price swings.
