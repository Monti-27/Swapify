# Getting Started

You don't need complex registration forms to access WeSwap's automation tools. All you need is a Web3 wallet supporting the Solana network and a small amount of SOL in your wallet to cover network fees.

#### 1. Wallet Requirements

For the best experience, we recommend using one of the following wallets:

* Phantom Wallet: (Most popular and recommended)
* Solflare: (Alternative secure option)

#### 2. Connecting Your Wallet

1. Open the WeSwap application in your browser.
2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button located at the top right corner of the screen.
3. Select your wallet provider from the pop-up window and approve the connection request.

#### 3. Getting to Know the Interface

Once connected, the Dashboard will load. Here you will see:

* Wallet Balance: Your real-time SOL and token balances.
* Active Trades: Your currently running automations or open orders.
* Mode Selection: The main menu where you can access WeSwap's exclusive trading modes (e.g., Boomerang Mode).
