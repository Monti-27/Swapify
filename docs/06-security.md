# Security & Infrastructure

The safety of user funds is WeSwap's top priority. Our platform is built on a "Non-Custodial" architecture.

## 1. Fund Control

WeSwap never takes custody of your funds.
* All transactions are executed directly from your wallet (Phantom/Solflare) via smart contracts.
* Your Private Keys are never stored on our servers.

## 2. Smart Contract Security

* **Pump.fun Standards:** Our token contract relies on Pump.fun's battle-tested standard contracts. Mint Authority is disabled, meaning no new tokens can ever be minted.
* **DApp Security:** Our website and trading bot are protected against external attacks via encrypted connections (SSL) and regular security scans.
