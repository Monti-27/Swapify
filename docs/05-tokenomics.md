# Tokenomics & Launch

At WeSwap, we stand against hidden private sales and unfair team allocations. That fits why we are launching our native utility token via **Pump.fun**, the most transparent and fair platform in the Solana ecosystem.

## 1. Launch Method: Pump.fun Fair Launch

* **Fair Distribution:** Everyone buys the token through the bonding curve mechanism at the same time. No presales, no VC allocations.
* **Liquidity Assurance:** Once the bonding curve is completed (hitting the Market Cap target), liquidity is automatically migrated to Raydium and burnt. This minimizes rug pull risks significantly.

## 2. Token Utility

The WeSwap token is not just for speculation; it is the fuel of our platform:
* **Fee Discounts:** Token holders enjoy reduced transaction fees on swaps and automation tools within WeSwap.
* **Access to Premium Modes:** Holders of a certain amount of tokens gain access to exclusive features like "Sniper Mode" or advanced "Boomerang Strategies."

## 3. Migration to Raydium

Upon completing the curve on Pump.fun, the token will be officially listed on Raydium DEX, integrating fully into the broader Solana DeFi ecosystem.
